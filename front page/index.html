What is QUADrilateral ?

QUADrilateral is an innovative idea to solve the real world problem of mainly the student domain. As even for a school student it's quite tough to remember all the forms and its details. Therefore, QUADrilateral solves the problem of every person who wants to keep record of the various forms they filled, by its super fascinating features like entering the exam detail ones and get the reminder notifications as per the need, can see the recent upcoming exam according to the filled details before, keep the record of the forms that are being filled, and many more. Now through QUAdrilateral none of the important exams can be missed.
